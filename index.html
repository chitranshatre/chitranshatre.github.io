About me

Where curiosity meets consciousness, every discovery opens doors to deeper understanding. I’m a passionate researcher exploring the universe’s energy—from droplets to cosmos—seeking hidden patterns that connect mind and soul.

During my PhD at IIT Madras, I specialized in fluid dynamics, CFD, heat transfer, and machine learning using OpenFOAM, Python, MATLAB, and C++. I discovered a new scaling law in surface tension-driven flows, blending theory, simulation, and experiments to reveal nature’s fingerprints. Mathematics is my compass connecting formulas to reality.

Currently, I work on DST projects advancing defense and heat pipe technologies. Beyond the lab, I enjoy badminton, cooking, and traveling across India—experiencing unity in diversity, much like the flows I study. I’m fascinated by the flow of consciousness in neural networks.

Open-source collaboration drives my passion—great discoveries come from sharing and building together. I welcome connection and collaboration in CFD, heat transfer, radiation, fuel cells, and machine learning.

Reach me at chitransh.atre@gmail.com to explore new mysteries and discover patterns together.
